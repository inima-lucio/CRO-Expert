## Mobile vs. Desktop
| Metric | Mobile | Desktop |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Traffic share | 65–75% | 25–35% |
| Conversion rate | 1.2–2.0% | 2.5–4.5% |
| AOV | 15–20% lower | Baseline |
| Session duration | Shorter | Longer |

**Implication**: If mobile CVR < 50% of desktop CVR, mobile UX is a primary issue.

## Social Proof Impact on CVR
| Element | CVR Uplift |
|---------|-----------|
| Displaying any reviews | +15–30% |
| Reviews with photos | +25–45% |
| >50 reviews vs. <10 | +35–65% |
| Response to negative reviews | +12% (trust) |
| Video testimonials | +30–50% on PDP |
